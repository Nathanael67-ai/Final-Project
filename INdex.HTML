About this coloring set!

The 150-pack coloring set exploded across the table like a rainbow grenade, its sharpened tips gleaming under the fluorescent lights of the kindergarten art room. Cheap plastic barrels rattled as tiny hands rummaged for "perfect flesh tone" (always missing) or that one specific green no other set ever got right. Broken wax skeletons littered the bottom.
